Spam Musubi

My recipe for making a sweet and salty spam musubi. A can of Spam is cut into 10 slices and each placed upon a bed of rice.

Ingredients

  • 5 cups of rice uncooked
  • 1 can of Spam
  • 5 sheets of nori, cut in half
  • 60ml shoyu
  • 5 tbsp white sugar
